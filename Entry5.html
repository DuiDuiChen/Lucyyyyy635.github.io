Mood Map

Colorful moods guide our paths through urban environments, from bustling downtown streets to serene corners, expressing layers of sentiment that add depth to our everyday lives.

Creating emotional maps is a deeply personal experience, mapping joy, nostalgia, and even moments of calmness across the cityscape. These maps are visual diaries of our relationship with space and emotion.

Each location tells a story of emotions tied to urban rhythm. From vibrant parks to cozy cafes, each site evokes memories and feelings unique to every individual.
