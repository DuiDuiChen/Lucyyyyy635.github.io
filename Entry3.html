Emotional Cartography: Mapping My Urban Journey

This project explores how emotions are geographically and visually represented in everyday life. My emotional map connects my favorite restaurants, dim sum stores, cafes, parks, art museums, and other meaningful places in the city. Each location is tied to a particular mood or emotion, creating a vibrant and evolving visual diary of my experiences.

Cafes that bring a sense of happiness and anticipation.

Parks that offer relaxation and a moment to connect with nature.

Art museums that fill me with inspiration and curiosity.

Inspiration and Thoughts

Inspired by my Google Maps pins, I marked all the places I love to visit in green, and they began to form a pattern. It sparked the idea of translating these moments into a map filled with emotions. By adding elements from my favorite spots, such as restaurants and cafes, I hope to create an emotional map that resonates with different experiences.
