Emotional Connections

Geographical Representation

"Emotional Map" explores the geographical and visual representation of emotions in daily life. Everyone has unique emotional responses to different places and environments, and visualizing them in the form of a map would be fascinating.

My initial idea is to connect my favorite restaurants, dessert shops, and cafes based on the joy and anticipation I feel each time I visit these places. Of course, I can add other elements later, such as parks, art museums, and interviews with classmates and friends. Ultimately, I will connect these places with emotions to create a map driven by different color emotions.

  • Color 2: Inspiration
